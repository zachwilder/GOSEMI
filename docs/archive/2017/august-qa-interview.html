What market demands prompted the development of the T5822?

Semiconductor makers need low-cost solutions for high-volume testing of price-sensitive memory ICs. The primary driver is the booming mobile-device market. While the PC marketâ€™s share of DRAM bit demand plunged to just 26 percent between 2009 and 2016, mobile DRAM bit share has grown more than 500 percent since 2009. How does the T5822 fit with other current memory testers in the T5800 family?

The T5822 is designed for economical wafer-level testing by IDMs/OSATs [integrated device manufacturers/outsourced semiconductor assembly and test providers] producing or testing both DRAMs and NAND flash memories.
