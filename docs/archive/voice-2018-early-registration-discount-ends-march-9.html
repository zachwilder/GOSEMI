VOICE 2018 Early Registration Discount Ends March 9

Events • February 2018 Issue

Now in its 12th year, the Advantest VOICE Developer Conference will be held in San Diego, California, on May 15-16, 2018, and in Hsinchu, Taiwan on May 23. Online registration is open with a 20-percent discount offered for the San Diego event through March 9.
